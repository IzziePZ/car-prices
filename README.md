<h1>Car Prices Project<h1>

<h2>Introduction<h2>

<p>This is one of the projects I had to do for my Data class. I downloaded the dataset from Kaggle and it includes the following fields: year, make, model, trim, body type, transmission type, VIN (Vehicle Identification Number), state of registration, condition rating, odometer reading, exterior and interior colors, seller information, Manheim Market Report (MMR) values, selling prices, and sale dates.<p>

<h2>Objectives<h2>

<p>The objective of this project was to identify the most and least popular cars, analyze the correlation between selling price and mileage to find a trend, and provide actionable insights for investments and strategies to improve sales of less popular cars.<p>
